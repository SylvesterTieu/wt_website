Vision

This project aims to create a residential space that blends modern comfort with artistic flair, meeting the urban population's pursuit of quality living. The design is inspired by natural light and spatial balance, emphasizing interaction between people and the environment.

Interior Design Concepts & Features

  • Minimalist yet warm, using modern styles combined with natural materials.
  • Spatial planning emphasizes openness and functionality.
  • Extensive use of natural light and low-energy lighting enhances sustainability.
  • Color scheme focuses on soft neutral tones to create a peaceful atmosphere.

Designer: Jia Wang — Award-winning designer focused on sustainability and aesthetics.

Exterior Design Concepts & Features

  • Modern residential design values focused on aesthetics, comfort, privacy, and functionality.
  • Detached, family-oriented homes with private yards, large windows for natural light, and high-quality materials.
  • Despite stylistic differences, each house emphasizes a balance between visual appeal and practical living, aligning with contemporary trends in home design.

Note: Exterior views are designed to complement the interior harmony and sustainability goals.
